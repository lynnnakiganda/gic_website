About Us

Geo Information Communication Ltd



Geo-Information Communication (GIC) Limited was incorporated in May 2002 in Kampala, Uganda. GIC is a Geospatial company with a focus on providing solutions and services in the areas of Aerail mapping using UAVs, Mobile mapping using GPS enabled devices, application development in Geographical Information Systems (GIS), as well as Big Data management.

The company is a pioneer company that introduced and spearheaded extensive use of in Uganda and dedicated its resources into championing the use of Geospatial technology among the ever-expanding field of applications and professionals. From the vast experience, we fully understand the priorities and needs of our clients and which puts us in a unique position to innovate and develop solutions tailored to the needs our clients.

This year marks 20 years of proven experience in undertaking national and international Projects that involve design and development of Geospatial Platforms, capacity development, support to surveying, Remote Sensing and Knowledge Management.

Vision

To be a leading regional organization in the provision of socially responsible and environmentally sustainable geospatial information services and solutions.

Mission

To provide innovative geospatial solutions to our clients while taking advantage of and maximizing the benefits from existing and emerging geospatial technologies and cloud services to solve mission-critical and real-world problems.

Partners



Esri Inc. (Environmental Systems Research Institute) is an international manufacturer and supplier of geographic information system (GIS) (web, mobile, online and enterprise) software solutions. The company is headquartered in Redlands, California, USA. The company has 10 regional offices in the U.S. and a network of 80+ international distributors, with about a million users in 200 countries.

GIC is a member of the Esri Partner Network which is a worldwide community of organizations that develop and deliver cutting-edge geospatial solutions and services based on Esri technology. As a partner, of about 20 years with Esri through Esri East Africa (the Regional Distributor based in Nairobi, Kenya), we offer a wide range of resources, opportunities and tools to help our clients plan and deliver innovative solutions and services through successful GIS implementations.



OPTRON (Pty) Limited is a subsidiary of the holding company OPTRON Group (Pty) Limited headquartered in Centurion, Gauteng, South Africa. The company supplies advanced positioning solutions, which assist in maximizing the user’s productivity and over-all profitability by revolutionizing their work processes. It also supplies and supports products in most countries across sub-Saharan Africa, directly and indirectly with the assistance of a select network of re-sellers and agents.

GIC has over 10 years of business partnership with OPTRON Pty Limited as a Reseller of Trimble products for the Ugandan market. Our solutions integrate a wide range of positioning technologies including GPS, laser, optical and inertial technologies, coupled with the latest application software and wireless communication technologies.




Tersus is a leading GNSS solutions provider whose offerings and services aim to make centimeter- precision positioning affordable for large scale deployments. Founded in 2014, the company has been a pioneer in design and deployment of GNSS RTK products to better cater for the need of target industries.

The product portfolio includes GNSS RTK, PPK OEM boards and receivers, David GNSS Survey Receivers, NeoRTK (GNSS Survey Systems), MatrixRTK (GNSS CORS Systems), AG Autosteer Systems and inertial navigation systems. Designed for ease of use, Tersus’s solutions support multi-GNSS and flexible interfaces for a variety of applications such as UAVs, surveying, mapping, precision agriculture, lane-level navigation, construction engineering and deformation monitoring.

The business partnership between TERSUS and GIC aims to service the high procession and yet affordable segment of the positioning market.

Autodesk, Inc. is an American multinational software corporation that makes software products and services for the architecture, engineering, construction, manufacturing, media, education, and entertainment industries. Autodesk is headquartered in San Francisco, California, and has offices worldwide. The company is committed to making its users successful with Autodesk solutions.

Autodesk software provides customers with the right tools to work, the ability to think flexibly, and the power to transform what actually needs making. The company is headquartered in San Francisco, California with over 12,600 employees and a total revenue of $4.39 billion.

Geo-Information Communication (GIC) Limited is a Top Tier Autodesk Partner, representing the top tier of authorized partners in the Autodesk reseller channel offering products and services such as AutoCAD, Revit, Civil 3D, SLD drawings, professional training and support.
