About Us

Geo Information Communication Ltd



Geo-Information Communication (GIC) Limited was incorporated in May 2002 in Kampala, Uganda. The company is an information technology-based company with a focus on providing solutions and services in the area of Geographical Information Systems (GIS), Global Positioning Systems (GPS) and Remote Sensing. GIC is dedicated to championing the use of Geographic Information Systems (GIS) among the ever-expanding field of applications and professionals with a focus on providing solutions and services in the areas of GIS and related applications. The company fully understands the priorities and technology needs of its clients and sets the pace for innovation, advanced technology and a strong commitment to said clients, using its experience and expertise by providing quality services on time with better value for money.
GIC has over 20 years of proven experience in undertaking national and international Projects that involve design and development of GIS Systems, capacity development in GIS, Surveying, Remote Sensing, Communication and Knowledge Management.

Vision

To be a leading regional organization in the provision of socially responsible and environmentally sustainable geospatial information services and solutions.

Mission

To provide innovative geospatial solutions to our clients to clients taking advantage of and maximizing the benefits from existing and emerging geospatial technologies and cloud services to solve mission-critical and real-world problems.
