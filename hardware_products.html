Hardware Products


GIC provides you with all your surveying hardware products that are of high quality and standards to ensure all your land surveying needs. Our products will ensure more productivity and efficiency with faster and automated work time by simplifying your daily tasks.


Products


Digital Levels

We provide digital levels used for land surveying projects to perform advanced and automatic height calculations on sites between two points. GIC can provide you with the tool you need to perform advanced surveying work with automated calculations with accurate readings for automation, efficiency and making work faster.

GNSS Surveying Systems

GNSS solutions and systems designed with the surveyor in mind. Backed by a legacy of GNSS technology leadership and surveying expertise, GIC provides surveyors and GIS professionals with reliable, innovative GNSS survey solutions from Optron that meet their distinct requirements. For more than 30 years, Optron has set the standard for positioning technologies, and intends to continue that tradition well into the future.

Total Stations

We provide you with total stations for modern surveying, building construction, and mining projects for data collection straight to your computer for different features and records. Surveyors and engineers are provided with high tech total stations for all your needs.

Handheld GPS Receivers

GIC delivers GPS Receivers to collect all your GPS data and information for surveyors, engineers or internal use to accurately calculate geographical locations. Field workers can employ these to make data collection faster and easier as they are state-of-the-art technology.

Unmanned Aircraft Systems (Drones)

We provide UAS air vehicles and associated equipment with remote piloting for your monitoring and accessing purposes in remote locations. GIC will provide you with these devices to meet all your needs together with training and maintenance services.

Accessories

GIC also provides accessories to surveying equipment and tools. You can reach out to us to acquire surveying and engineering equipment accessories to compliment your equipment. Look no further, we can provide you with any needed accessories, provide training and installation for the equipment.
