INTERGEO 2022

In Essen, Germany on 18-20 October 2022

INTERGEO is the world‘s number one community platform for its field. INTERGEO focuses on information, professional exchange and personal encounters at eye level. It is THE event for forward-looking geo applications.

An important part of digitalisation is the question of „Where?“: Linking information with parameters such as en- vironment, time and condition is essential in the digitisation of processes. This is exactly where geodata come into play. Our real world becomes three-dimensional and digitally avai- lable with geodata. This is a source that makes a multitude of innovative applications and technology trends such as re- ality capturing, positioning technologies, machine learning, Building Information Modelling (BIM), digital planning and building, smart city, UAV, drones and smart surveying possib- le in the first place. Around 450 national and international companies are expected to attend INTERGEO EXPO 2022 in Essen, which, with its special areas for the commercial use of drones (INTERAERIAL SOLUTIONS) and in the field of geoin- novations, will represent the entire range of processes and solutions in the context of digitalisation.
Be there! We look forward to seeing you!

Training

GIS Training

These courses will teach you what a GIS is and what you can do with it. Working with various components of the ArcGIS platform and software, you will create GIS maps, explore and analyze the data behind the maps, and learn easy methods to share your maps and analysis results, working with different data types, geodatabases, mobile mapping and how to apply standard cartographic workflow to produce high-quality maps for both print and online use. By the end of the courses, you will have a solid understanding of how GIS maps and ArcGIS tools are used to visualize real-world features, discover patterns, obtain information, and communicate that information to others.

read more

AutoDesk Training

Whether you are a novice, expert, student, or professional, we have a training path for you. Your learning and training options range from free tips and tricks, videos, and self-service courses to paid services from GIC, an authorized AutoDesk Training Center.



read more

Remote Sensing Training

These courses introduce the principles of remote sensing for earth observation, remote sensing platforms and sensors, data acquisition, storage and processing. These are designed to give you a comprehensive understanding on the application of remote sensing and GIS in solving the research problems. By the end of the courses, you should be able to use remote sensing (Satellite images and Field data) and GIS in their future research work. Remote sensing is used in numerous fields, including geography, land surveying and most Earth science disciplines for multiple purposes like military, intelligence, commercial, economic, planning, and humanitarian applications, among others.

read more

Survey Equipment Training

These courses involve professionals at GIC teaching the basic applications of the different survey equipment and tools at our disposal. These include sensors, systems, devices and technology to collect and acquire the spatial data. We can provide you with the equipment for purchase as well as the necessary training on how to install, apply and use this equipment for modern surveying, building construction, and mining projects.

Current Projects

Ministry of Energy and Mineral Development

Current Project: Electricity for Rural Transformation for Ministry of Energy and Mineral Development

This project involves the collection of data using android devices (tablets) where information from installed meters in houses, is transferred to servers on a daily basis, involving 7 service providers like KISL, UMEME and others. The data collected includes the Name, Meter Number and Location and this will be used for budgeting purposes by the government and service providers.

China National Offshore Oil Corporation (CNOOC Uganda Limited)

Current Project: Tilting the Land Acquired at Buhuka Parish i Kikuube District for the Kingfisher Development Project for China National Offshore Oil Corporation (CNOOC Uganda Limited)

CNOOC Uganda Limited has implemented 3 distinct Resettlement Action Plans (RAPs) to acquire land in support of infrastructure such as the access roads, permanent camp, material/supply base, airstrip, Central Processing Facility, Well Pads, infield flow lines linking well pads to the CPF, buffers, Feeder pipeline to evacuate the crude oil from Buhuka to the delivery point at Kabaale, spoil areas and burrow pits, all of which are required for the project development. The assignment involves opening boundaries of registered land, mutating/subdividing titles and processing titles for the project components at Buhuka.
