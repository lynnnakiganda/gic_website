AutoCAD

Architects, engineers, and construction professionals use AutoCAD for the following:

    • AutoCAD Revit

    Autodesk® Revit® Structure BIM software for structural engineering provides tools specifically built for structural design and analysis. With the advantage of BIM, Revit® Structure helps to improve multi-discipline coordination of structural design documentation, minimize errors and enhance collaboration between structural engineers and extended project team members, such as architects, MEP engineers, and owners.

    Autodesk® Revit® MEP BIM software is for mechanical, electrical, and plumbing (MEP) engineers. It supports more accurate and efficient building systems design projects from concept through construction.It is used to:

  • Design building systems more accurately using coordinated, consistent information inherent in the intelligent Revit® MEP model.
  • Analyze for efficiency with integrated analysis earlier in the process.
  • Keep documentation coordinated and consistent with parametric change management technology.
  • Deliver 3D models and documentation to support the building lifecycle.
    • AutoCAD Civil 3D

    AutoCAD®  Civil 3D ®  software is the building information modeling (BIM) solution for civil engineering helps project teams explore transportation, land development, and environmental projects digitally before they are built. The creation of more coordinated and consistent information helps enable delivery of higher- quality documentation, visualizations, and 3D models for construction.

    Civil 3D® is a design software that empowers civil engineers to realize tomorrow’s infrastructure, today by offering the following functionalities:

  • Work with a model-based environment for better design decisions and project quality.
  • Streamline documentation with a design-driven approach to plans production.
  • Harness the power of BIM (Building Information Modeling) for greater design coordination and project collaboration.
